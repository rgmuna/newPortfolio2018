Goals:

With a creative mindset, passion for learning, strong technical and troubleshooting skill set, and a variety of work experiences, my goal is to pursue a career as a front end web developer where I can build websites and make tangible contributions towards an exciting product.

Current Job:

Since October 2016, I've spent nearly 100% of time at working on web development. Starting with a brand new company website, I designed and built Preston Cinema System's consumer-facing, business website from scratch, including a build system tutorial and quote generator/shopping cart using modern web technologies. Doing so has increased page views by over 50% on average with a bounce rate of around 33%. Aside form maintaining the current website and adding new features, I'm continuing to optimize processes in the company through the use of web apps.

History:
